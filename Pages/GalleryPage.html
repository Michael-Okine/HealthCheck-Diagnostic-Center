Who we are

Healthcheck Diagnostic Center is a leading provider of clinical pathology and medical diagnostic services, with the ambition to become a trusted diagnostic services provider delivering world-class pathology standards to patients and medical communities across the continent. Healthcheck Diagnostic Center is a joint venture established in 2019 through a strategic partnership between an international healthcare diagnostics group and a reputable clinical pathology organization in Sub-Saharan Africa,
combining global expertise with strong regional experience to deliver accurate and reliable medical diagnostic services.

Founders Profile

Healthcheck Diagnostic Center was founded by Michael Okine, a student of Ghana Communication Technology University (GCTU). The idea behind establishing the center was driven by his passion for healthcare innovation and his desire to contribute to improved medical diagnostic services within the community. As a student with a strong interest in technology and healthcare systems, Michael Okine envisioned Healthcheck Diagnostic Center as a modern diagnostic facility that combines accuracy, efficiency, and patient-centered care. His academic background has influenced the center’s focus on adopting digital solutions and modern laboratory practices to support quality healthcare delivery.
