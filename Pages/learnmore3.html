Essence preventive Health checkup services

A comprehensive health checkup is the best way to discover most medical conditions at an early and treatable stage. Early detection of a disease enables early management and treatment of the condition which in turn reduces the risk of complications. Health checkup can result in you our cherished customers deciding to make positive improvements in your lives. Essence has the best health checkup packages to help you make lifestyle changes that can impact your health. Essence believes that with regular health checkups, we are more informed about the condition of our bodies, the irregularities involved and the efforts we will have to make towards avoiding severe health issues and keeping track of our health. Our health check programme is designed to carefully assess your overall health.

Our radiologists and sonographers will make reports and films (images)
ready in an hour after examinations.
